about ke ♡

♡ hi, i’m ke.

i’m in my early twenties, finishing my bachelor’s degree this august, studying for the lsat, and slowly building the life i used to only daydream about.

i’ve always been drawn to beautiful things early 2000s films, glossy magazines, late night thoughts written in pink notebooks, fashion that tells a story, and women who reinvent themselves quietly.

this blog is where all of those things come together. ☆

my path hasn’t been traditional. as a firstgeneration student, i didn’t have the privilege of moving through life slowly or perfectly. i had to work, figure things out as i went, and keep believing in myself even during the hardest seasons of my life.

and somehow, i’m still here. still dreaming bigger. ♡

“girl on a mission” was created for girls who want more for themselves girls who are soft, ambitious, creative, emotional, stylish, intelligent, uncertain, and determined all at once.

i’m especially passionate about fashion law, storytelling, femininity, and creating a future that feels authentic instead of expected.

♡ this space is part journal, part moodboard, part love letter to becoming.

whether you’re here for law school diaries, fashion thoughts, early 2000s nostalgia, or reminders to keep going, i hope you leave feeling a little more inspired to believe in your own future.

you are allowed to become everything you dreamed of.
